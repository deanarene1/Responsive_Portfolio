About Me


My name is Deana Baker and I am a single mother to the sassiest three year old on the planet. I have spent the last few years of my life working as an independent contractor/brand ambassador for a ton of companies and also have bartended on the side.

Originally, I wanted to be a nurse and took up two years of community college doing general coursework before I realized it wasnt what I wanted to do. I am now ready to embark on my career as a software engineer, as I have finally found something that I love. I am currently a student through the University of Arizona at the coding bootcamp. Upon graduation in October of 2018, I plan to finish my associates degree in computer science since my general college coursework is already completed.

My hobbies include hiking, reading, writing, Grey's Anatomy or Harry Potter marathons, yoga, cross-training at the gym and now more recently, coding and thinking of new ideas for future apps I can build to help people or solve a problem.
